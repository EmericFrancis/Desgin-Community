Slack

Get started on Slack for collaborating and communicating

Create a Slack account

  • Go to Slack.com and sign up with your work email address for a free account.

  • You'll then be taken to a new page where you can click 'Find your Slack workspace'.

  • Click this link and you'll get a confirmation email.

  • If you don't have remote access to your outlook on your Macbook, you'll need to forward any confirmation emails to an email account that you can access on your Macbook.

Join Slack workspaces

  • You'll need to join the following workspaces

  • landregistry.slack.com
  • ukgovernmentdigital.slack.com
