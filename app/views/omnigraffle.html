Omnigraffle

To enable running of a diagramming application

Apply for licence

  • To request installation of the software you'll need to raise a 'Request for Service' via 'mySelfService' on HMLR's intranet.

  • Go to the 'Start' menu and select 'All Programs'. 'mySelfService' is under #F6 MY REQUEST HUB'.

  • You'll then see the main option screen of the mySelfService. Click on 'Standard IT request' and then type 'software' to find the 'Software install/ Renew/ Removal or Transfer Request' option.

  • Click on this option, fill out your details and click 'Raise Request'.

  • You'll receive an email with the request number which you can use in the Marvel system to check progress of your request.

Download Omnigraffle

  • Go to www.omnigroup.com/omnigraffle and click on the centre 'Free Trial' option.

  • This will download a 14 day free trial for you to install on the Macbook whilst you wait for the RFS to complete.

  • Once your RFS has completed you'll be provided with an exchange code.

  • You will then need to visit application-systems.co.uk/exchange and input this code. You'll then be emailed a personal licence key which you can input into Omnigraffle.

  • Open Omnigraffle and click 'Omnigraffle - Licences' to input your personal licence key and make your version of Omnigraffle official.
