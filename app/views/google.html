Google

To enable access to HM Land Registry Google groups and drives

Create Google account

  • If you have not done so you'll need a Google account set up with your HM Land Registry email account.

  • Go to SignUpWithoutGmail and create an account using your email in the form-

  • firstname.lastname@landregistry.gov.uk

Join Google groups

  • You should then use your new account to join the GDS design community. Go to the following location and apply for membership.

  • Digital Service Designers

Join Google drive

  • You should request access to the following google drives -

  • HM Land Registry

  • User Centered Design
