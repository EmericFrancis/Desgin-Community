Google

To enable access to HM Land Registry Google groups and drives

Create a Google account

  • You'll need to set up a Google account with your HM Land Registry email address.
  • Go to 'Sign Up Without Gmail' and create an account using your email in the format:
    firstname.lastname@landregistry.gov.uk

Join Google groups

  • Use your new Google account to join the GDS design community.

Join Google drives

  • You should request access to the HM Land Registry and the User Centered Design drives.
