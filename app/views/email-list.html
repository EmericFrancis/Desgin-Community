Get added to email distribution lists

To receive emails addressed to the team

Create Request for service

  • To join the group email distrubution list you'll need to raise a Request for Service via 'MySelfService'.
  • Go to the 'Start' menu and select 'All Programs'. 'MySelfService' is under #F6 MY REQUEST HUB.
  • You'll then see the main option screen of the mySelfService. Click on 'Standard IT request' and then type 'email' to find the 'Modify Email Distrubtion List' option.
  • Click on this option, fill out your details and click 'Raise Request'. The name of the distrubution list is 'Design and content design'
  • You'll receive an email with the request number which you can use in the Marvel system to check progress of your request.
