To enable access to Office 365 on a macbook

Create Request for service

  • To access Office 360 from your Macbook you will need to raise a Request for Service via 'MySelfService'.

  • Go to the 'Start' menu and select 'All Programs'. 'MySelfService' is under #F6 MY REQUEST HUB'.

  • You will then see the main option screen of the mySelfService. Click on 'Standard IT request' and then type 'remote' to find the 'Remote Access request' option.

  • Click on this option, fill out your details and click 'Raise Request'.

  • You will receive an email with the request number which you can use in the Marvel system to check progress of your request.

  • Within 15 days you should be able to pick up your 'Entrust' device for second factor authentication.

Accessing your Land Registry mailbox from a personal computer or device

  • To access your Land Registry mailbox from a personal computer or device, please navigate to https://login.microsoftonline.com and enter your land registry email

  • When you click into the Password box to type your password you'll be automatically redirected to another web page.

  • YThe redirection will take you to federate.landregistry.gov.uk. Enter your domain password (the one you log into your Land Registry computer with) and select "Sign in".

  • Second Factor Authentication will be required every time you access your Land Registry mailbox from a personal computer or device.

  • Please generate a code from your Entrust Security token by holding the button down for a few seconds, then enter the code in the box on the web page and select "Submit".

  • You can use one of the other authentication options, but only if you have been pre-enrolled and configured for them.

  • Once you have been successfully authenticated you will be re-directed to the Office 365 dashboard. Click the "Mail" tile to access your work email.
