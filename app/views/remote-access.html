Apply for remote access

To enable access to Office 365 on a Macbook

Create a Request for service

  • To access Office 360 from your Macbook you'll need to raise a Request for Service via 'mySelfService'.
  • Go to the 'Start' menu and select 'All Programs'. 'mySelfService' is under #F6 MY REQUEST HUB'.
  • You'll then see the main option screen of the mySelfService. Click on 'Standard IT request' and then type 'remote' to find the 'Remote Access request' option.
  • Click on this option, fill out your details and click 'Raise Request'.
  • You'll receive an email with the request number. You can use this in Marvel to check the progress of your request.
  • Within 15 days you should be able to pick up your 'Entrust' token for second factor authentication.

Accessing your HM Land Registry mailbox from a personal computer or device.

  • To access your HM Land Registry mailbox from a personal computer or device, please navigate to Microsoft and enter your land registry email
  • Clicking in the password box will automatically redirect you
  • Once here, enter your domain password (the one you log into your HM Land Registry computer with) and select "Sign in".
  • Two Factor Authentication will be required every time you access your Land Registry mailbox from a personal computer or device.
  • Please generate a code from your Entrust Security token by holding the button down for a few seconds, then enter the code in the box on the web page and select "Submit".
  • You can use one of the other authentication options, but only if you have been pre-enrolled and configured for them.
  • Once you have been successfully authenticated, you'll be re-directed to the Office 365 dashboard. Click the "Mail" tile to access your work email.
